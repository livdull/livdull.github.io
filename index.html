about

olivia tull

is an artist and filmmaker born and raised in los angeles, california. she is known for working in both digital and analog, as well as re-working found footage, experimental films, music videos, and branching out into writing and directing some narrative based projects with themes consisting of ritual motifs, existence, identities, and sound work. graduated from the california institute of the arts with a BFA in film and video.

cicatrix 2024

using found footage from coni beeson’s haunting film, “unfolding”, olivia tull re-curates young people depicted in various stages of finding intimacy, sacrifice, and rituals; using chemical processes and digital optical printing.

ROAD STORM KILL CHASE 2024

ROAD STORM KILL CHASE, using footage filmed on a road trip with a dear friend, a page in my notes app. treated as half a nonsensical made-up diary, partially in response to things she had said but also whatever other things came to mind, some based on truth, and some totally fictional. I played with a sense of anonymity in identity, and my characters; as well as a choppy, non-linear editing style. True or false.
